FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What types of exercises are included in the video courses? The video courses include a variety of exercises such as cardio, strength training, yoga, and HIIT workouts.
Are the video courses suitable for beginners? Yes, the video courses are designed to cater to all fitness levels, including beginners. Modifications and progressions are provided to ensure everyone can follow along.
How long are the video courses? The duration of each video course varies, ranging from 20 minutes to 60 minutes. You can choose courses based on your availability and preferences.
Can I access the video courses on mobile devices? Yes, the website is fully responsive and you can access the video courses on any device, including smartphones and tablets.
Is personal training available? Yes, I offer personal training sessions for those who prefer one-on-one guidance and support. Please contact me for more information.
